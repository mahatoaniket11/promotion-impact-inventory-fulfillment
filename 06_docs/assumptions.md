# Project Assumptions

## Demand Behavior

Normal daily demand per product ranges from 5–30 units.

Promotional campaigns increase demand by approximately
2x to 4x depending on discount percentage.

## Promotion Behavior

Promotions last between 3 and 14 days.

Discount range: 5%–40%.

## Inventory Management

Reorder point = 25% of average inventory level.

Lead time for restocking is assumed to be 5 days.

## Dataset Design

Data is synthetically generated to simulate retail supply chain
behavior in a controlled environment.

The dataset will include:

Products
Promotions
Orders
Inventory
