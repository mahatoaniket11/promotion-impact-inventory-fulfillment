# Project Title

Promotion Impact on Inventory & Fulfillment

## Business Problem

Retail promotions increase demand but can disrupt supply chain operations.
Unexpected demand spikes may cause stockouts, delayed fulfillment, and lost sales.

The challenge is to quantify how promotional campaigns impact demand variability,
inventory health, and fulfillment performance.

## Key Business Question

How do marketing promotions affect:

- demand spikes
- stockout risk
- fulfillment performance?

## Stakeholders

Marketing Team
Supply Chain Planning
Warehouse Operations
Finance

## Success Metrics

Promotion Lift %
Stockout Rate
Inventory Turnover
Fulfillment SLA Compliance
Revenue Impact

## Project Scope

This project analyzes simulated retail data to evaluate the operational
impact of promotional campaigns.

Scope includes:

- demand analysis during promotions
- inventory depletion modeling
- fulfillment performance evaluation

## Expected Outcome

Insights into how promotions affect supply chain performance and
recommendations for inventory planning during promotional campaigns.
